Welcome to the AIM UPGRADE

Aim Upgrade is a website game that helps inprove the aim of players/gamers.
Players have a 60 second timer to pop ballons that appear in random grid boxes every half a second.
Players must click on the balloon image to increase the score number.
We will make recommendations based on your score so do your best.
Games like Valorant, Overwatch 2 and Call of Duty are aim oriented.
Improving your aim will lead to higher stats on these games.
We recommend using an external mouse for this game.

Login to access your profile.

Register a profile to save your score.

The score of the logged in player will be displayed on the score webpage.
